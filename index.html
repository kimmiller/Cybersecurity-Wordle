Cybersecurity Awareness Month Wordle Game

How to Play

Guess the cybersecurity-themed word of the day. You have six tries. After each guess, the color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word.

  • Green: Correct letter in the correct position
  • Yellow: Correct letter in the wrong position
  • Gray: Letter not in the word

Each word comes with a clue and an educational tip to help you learn more about cybersecurity.
